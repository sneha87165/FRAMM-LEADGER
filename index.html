FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Farm2Fork

How does blockchain ensure food safety?

Blockchain creates an immutable record of every transaction in the supply chain. Once data is recorded, it cannot be altered or deleted, ensuring complete transparency and preventing fraud.

Is the platform easy to use for farmers?

Yes! We've designed the platform with simplicity in mind. Farmers can register products and generate QR codes with just a few taps on their mobile phones.

What devices are supported?

Farm2Fork works on all modern devices including smartphones (iOS & Android), tablets, and desktop computers. We also have dedicated mobile apps for better experience.

How much does it cost?

We offer a free starter plan for small farmers. Professional plans start at ₹999/month. Enterprise pricing is customized based on your needs.

Can I try before committing?

Absolutely! We offer a 30-day free trial for all paid plans. No credit card required to start.

What support is available?

We provide 24/7 email support for all users. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority phone support and dedicated account managers.
